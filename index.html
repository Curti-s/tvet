About us

ORACON CONSTRUCTION AND ENGINEERING CO. LTD was registered in 1996 and inco-operated in 2004 as a construction and engineering company undertaking integrated projects in design, fabrication, erection and construction of structural steel structures, buildings, civil engineering, power transmission, telecommunication engineering, agricultural and environmental engineering.

Oracon Construction and Engineering Co. Ltd is a medium sized firm with a pool of well motivated manpower backed with modern equipment and technology for execution of various tasks .One of the directors is a trained and experienced construction engineer in charge of overseeing all operations in the firm on full time basis. Oracon construction and Engineering Ltd undertakes projects regionery in East and Central Africa with a focus of going global in the near future .
